Clickable Map with Parking sensor data and bar charts

 

In this demo you will learn how to recreate this clickable map that selects parking bays according to how close the bays are to the point selected on the map.  The demo also presents you with two bar charts showing the hourly availability and the daily availability.  The results are aggregated over the last 28 days.  Click the picture below or here to access the demo.

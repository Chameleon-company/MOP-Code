What is Melbourne Open Data Project (MOP)



The City of Melbourne initialted MOP with Deakin to support the user of there open Date by business, researchers and software developers. Open Data is a component of their smart cities' stratrgy.

Our project aims to develop an educational platform that utilises the City of Melbourne's Open Data to showcase the practical applications of Open Data to cater to the needs of various stakeholders, such as industry professionals, government entities and academic researchers.

Our long-term goal is to use Data to facilitate innovation and informed solutions to contemporary challenges across the City of Melbourne council area.

The Vehicle Classification Project aims to work towards creating machine learning
models to detect events such as speeding vehicles or vehicle accidents in real-time.
Theses model could leverage various data sources, such as image or video feeds,
sensor data from vehicles or infrastructure, or audio data, to identify events as they occur.
The goal is to gather data for further analysis with the hope to infrom decision-making in
areas such asimprove things such asroad safety or response times.

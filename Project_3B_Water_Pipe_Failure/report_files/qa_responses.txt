Q: What causes cast iron pipe failure?

[Cast Iron Pipe Assessment Standards]
Pipes before 1970 are highest risk. Common failures: circumferential breaks, pitting, graphitization. Replace cast iron in corrosive soil with more than 2 previous breaks.

Q: How do soil conditions affect pipe corrosion?

[NACE/AWWA Soil Corrosion Standards]
Soil resistivity below 2000 ohm-cm is highly corrosive. pH below 5.5 or above 8.5 increases risk. High moisture with clay is worst combination for metallic pipes.
